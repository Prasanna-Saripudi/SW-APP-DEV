Prevention

Five basic rules to be followed:

  1. STAY home
  2. KEEP a safe distance
  3. WASH hands often
  4. COVER your cough
  5. SICK? Call the helpline

Do's

  • Wash your hands frequently for 20+ sec with soap and water frequently
  • Stay at home and limit contact with others
  • Clean the frequently touched spaces in your home
  • Seek medical help if you’re showing symptoms like coughing, fever, breathing difficulty
  • Follow social distancing

    • Dont's

  • Don’t go to crowded areas
  • Don’t touch your face
  • Don’t be in close contact with others
  • Don’t ignore symptoms
  • Don’t leave your home, especially if sick
  • Don't touch public surfaces

    • COVID-19 Flattening the curve:
    The enforcement of social distancing and lockdowns is in an attempt to flatten the curve, or the amount of people who contract this disease.
